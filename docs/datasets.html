Below you can find list of all the datasets and models used in the benchmark. Unfortunately we are not able to give you ready-to-use models because of licensing of some of the model sharing websites, so in most cases we share links to the original sources. Note that some models were manually preprocessed before the rendering.

  • Notre Dame
  • Pantheon
  • Reichstag
  • St. Peter's Square
  • St. Vitus Cathedral
